✦ The higher self

What is the
higher self?

Your higher self is truly you. It is who you are at your core — your essence, unclouded by fear, by ego, by the noise of everyday life. It is you with all the love you have for yourself. The most honest, most loving, most clear version of you that exists.

This part of you can see around the corner. While you navigate daily life from where you stand — with its uncertainty, its doubt, its competing voices — your higher self already sees a few steps ahead. It knows what's waiting for you down the path you can't yet see.

It communicates with you constantly. Not through words, but through your emotions. When something excites you — truly, unexpectedly lights you up — that's your higher self pointing. There's something there for you. Something to discover, somewhere you need to be.

That feeling isn't random. It's guidance. Most of us have just forgotten how to listen to it.

I work exclusively with your energy and soul's wisdom. Every message that comes through is specific, personal, and meant for you at this exact moment in your life.

A session with me is a conversation between you, your higher self, your spirit guides, and the universe. I receive and translate what comes through.

✦ What this isn't

A few things worth knowing.

I don't communicate with people who have passed. I'm a psychic channel, not a medium. If a specific message is meant to come through from a loved one who has passed, it surely can — however I am not specialised in mediumship readings if this is the main goal of your reading.

I don't channel other living people without their consent. If you're hoping to find out how a specific person feels about you, this isn't the right fit. I can however tell you how your higher self feels about this person, truly.

I don't predict the future in absolute terms — no one can — but indications of what lies ahead can sometimes be seen from where you currently stand. I can see how your future may look if you stay aligned with your purpose and the path you're on right now. That said, you always have free will — you can change the trajectory of your life at any moment. What I can possibly offer — if this is something you are meant to know — is a picture of where you're headed from here, so you can choose consciously.

✦
A note on symbols, numbers, and things that don't immediately make sense

Not everything that comes through will resonate right away — and that's completely normal. If you hear a symbol, a number, a name, or a message that doesn't click in the moment, write it down anyway. Keep it somewhere you'll find it again. These are often signs. Their meaning may reveal itself days, weeks, or even months later — through a conversation, a moment, something you see or feel. Stay open. The message is already on its way to you.

✦ About Nicole

I help you hear what your soul
has been trying to tell you.

I'm Nicole — a psychic channel. I didn't grow up calling myself psychic. I grew up calling it a feeling. An instinct. A knowing I couldn't always explain but couldn't ignore either.

My background in depth psychology gave me a framework for understanding the inner world — how we work, why we make the choices we make, what drives us beneath the surface. That foundation never left me. What I do now sits right at the intersection of the psychological and the spiritual.

In the summer of 2025, I walked into a workshop near my home that claimed to teach channelling in 90 minutes. I went out of curiosity. Within that session, I channelled for the stranger sitting next to me — and accurately remote-viewed his apartment in detail. In that moment I knew: there is something here, and it's real.

That experience led me to a five-month training course with a lifelong psychic medium. I graduated at the end of 2025, having completed numerous readings throughout my training and beyond. At the start of 2026 I opened my practice — and I haven't looked back.

I love the process of channelling. It gives me deep joy — and I always leave a session feeling full, not depleted. Seeing how grateful people are, witnessing the moment something lands for them, being reminded again and again that we are all connected on an energetic level that most of us can't fully see — it still blows my mind every time. I am still exploring this world myself, and I am in awe every single time I do a reading. That sense of wonder hasn't left me, and I hope it never does.
